About Me

I am a Computer Science Engineering student with a strong foundation in programming and problem-solving, and a growing interest in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I have begun building core AI/ML concepts and exploring how intelligent systems can address real-world challenges.

My experience is driven by project-based learning, including full-stack web development projects such as Aurelia, a jewellery e-commerce website, and CLU (College Life Unplugged), a hackathon-built platform for tracking campus events and deadlines. I have also completed a data science and predictive modeling project, working with data preprocessing, exploratory analysis, and model evaluation using Python.

Additionally, I built an IoT-based Smart Bio-Sand Filtration System for real-time water quality monitoring and completed a research paper on Quantum Sensing, strengthening my analytical and practical problem-solving skills. I am comfortable with tools like VS Code, Jupyter Notebook, Git/GitHub, Excel, and Figma, and have enhanced my skills through Coursera and Forage certifications.

I aim to grow further in AI, Machine Learning, software development, and data-driven technologies, and I'm eager to collaborate on innovative and impactful projects.
